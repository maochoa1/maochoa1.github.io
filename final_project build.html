B64d Build

The B64d kit still in the box
The B64d kit

The build for the B64d was not easy. The screws that hold the differntials were really tight and 2 screws were ruined.
The rest was very simple because everthing fit and finished smoothly.
The kit includes everthing except wheels, tires, motor,esc,and battery.
I chose a team powers 6.5t modified motor with a team powers esc. For tires I chose jconcpets spacebars.
