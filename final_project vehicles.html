Team Associated B5m


The Team Associated B5m Factory Lite kit was my first competition racing car.
I first raced it during the winter of 2016 with only some success.
After I installed a 3d printed laydown transmission with a new motor and did some shock adjustments
I started winning alot more races and I am the current Points leader in a series.

Team Associated B44.3/B64d


My B44.3 was only raced for about 3 months. I bought it pre owned with a powerful modified motor.
It handled very well in the tight corners but quickly surfaced a overheatng issue.
I recently bought a new B64d kit that I built and does not have the overheating issue since it is lighter and more efficient.
This new buggy also handles and accelerates even better than the old buggy.
